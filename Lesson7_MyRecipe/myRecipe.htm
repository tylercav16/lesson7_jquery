Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker
  • 3 eggs separated
  • 1 teaspoon grated lime zest
  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed (optional)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Instructions

Preheat oven to 250.

In a large glass or metal bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

In a medium bowl, beat egg yolks, then mix in condesned milk, lime rind, and lime juice. Mix well.

Fold mixture into beaten egg whites and pour into graham cracker crust.

Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.

Chill before serving. If desired, garnish with whipped topping. Enjoy!
